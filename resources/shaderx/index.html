ShaderX/GPU Pro/GPU Zen Books

 See Wolfgang Engel's blog about these books for more information, including extended abstracts and excerpts. Extensive parts of many of these books are available for reading on Google Books, as shown in the links below. The first few ShaderX series books are free for download. Wolfgang Engel, the series editor, has worked with the publisher and authors to allow this release.

Direct3D ShaderX: Vertex and Pixel Shader Tips and Tricks, edited by Wolfgang Engel, Wordware Publishing, June 2002, download for free.
ShaderX2: Introductions and Tutorials with DirectX 9.0, edited by Wolfgang Engel, Wordware Publishing, Nov. 2003, download for free, also code download and notes (Amazon Look Inside).
ShaderX2: Shader Programming Tips and Tricks with DirectX 9.0, edited by Wolfgang Engel, Wordware Publishing, Nov. 2003, download for free, also code download and notes.
ShaderX3: Advanced Rendering with DirectX and OpenGL, edited by Wolfgang Engel, Charles River Media, Nov. 2004 (Table of Contents).
ShaderX4: Advanced Rendering Techniques, edited by Wolfgang Engel, Charles River Media, Jan. 2006 (Table of Contents, errata).
ShaderX5: Advanced Rendering Techniques, edited by Wolfgang Engel, Charles River Media, Dec. 2006 (Table of Contents, errata).
ShaderX6: Advanced Rendering Techniques, edited by Wolfgang Engel, Charles River Media, Feb. 2008 (Table of Contents, errata, Amazon Look Inside).
ShaderX7: Advanced Rendering Techniques, edited by Wolfgang Engel, Charles River Media, March 2009 (Table of Contents, errata).
